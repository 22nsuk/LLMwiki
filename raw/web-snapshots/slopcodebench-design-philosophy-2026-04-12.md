# Snapshot: SlopCodeBench design philosophy
- Capture date: 2026-04-12
- URL: https://www.scbench.ai/design-philosophy

## Key excerpts
- Iterative evaluation is presented as the right framing for long-horizon software engineering.
- The site emphasizes three design choices: no prescribed interfaces, no explicit test suite, and black-box / language-agnostic evaluation.
- The page explicitly links iterative evaluation to the difficulty of maintaining agent-generated code and to structural slop.

## Why this snapshot exists
The snapshot translates the paper's benchmark results into a design stance useful for wiki maintenance policy.
