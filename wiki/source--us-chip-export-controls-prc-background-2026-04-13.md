## Summary
이 BIS 문서는 2022년 10월과 2023년 10월 미국의 대중국 advanced computing / semiconductor manufacturing export-control 체계를 한 페이지에서 정리한 background surface다. 핵심은 advanced chip parameter rule, semiconductor manufacturing equipment control, circumvention prevention, arms-embargo country expansion, U.S. persons restriction 같은 장치가 `누가 어떤 compute rail에 접근할 수 있는가`를 정책적으로 규정한다는 점이다.

## Why it matters
현재 AI compute control corpus는 장기 계약, sovereign procurement, memory bottleneck에 더 강하다. 이 문서는 같은 질문을 `정책 perimeter` 쪽에서 보강해, compute control이 계약과 공급망만이 아니라 수출통제 rulemaking과 license architecture로도 정의된다는 점을 보여 준다.

## Key points
- BIS positions 2022 and 2023 rules as the core background for PRC advanced-computing controls.
- restricted-chip logic is tied to performance thresholds and performance density, not just branding.
- circumvention prevention expands the problem from direct exports to subsidiaries, transshipment, and diversion networks.
- semiconductor manufacturing equipment controls are framed as a separate but linked layer.
- U.S. persons restrictions and due-diligence obligations make compute control an organizational compliance issue as well as a product issue.

## Limitations / caveats
- this is a BIS explainer page, not a complete legal or economic analysis of downstream effects.
- it is strongest for policy architecture and terminology, weaker for market impact and enforcement outcomes.
- later 2026 rule changes can supersede parts of the background framing, so this page should be read as baseline context rather than the last word.
